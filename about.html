About Us

Blockchain Products & Services

Avarch is a software consultancy company providing blockchain products and services.

From blockchain media publishing to blockchain explorer services. Avarch is fostering the next generation developers to learn, contribute and expand the horizon of the blockchain ecosystem .

Our product includes EtherWorld, EIPsInsight (Page under development), helps thousands of mainstream users to follow the latest changes of the blockchain and enables them to contribute to the growing ecosystem.

Avarch is a leading software company providing services to the Ethereum Blockchain. We engage potential developers in learning the concept of blockchain development, providing the opportunity to contribute to novice projects. We publish technical blogs, produce ELI5 videos and share in-depth research report on blockchain protocols.

"Blockchain is a very deep subject. Keep building and maintain consistency in learning the process. Everyone can make a difference."

Our Founder

Pooja Ranjan, became part of the blockchain community in 2016 by stumbling upon the Ethereum blockchain. Founded Avarch LLC in 2017 with [EtherWorld](https://etherworld.co/) as her first project to create awareness about blockchain technology. Publishing technical blogs, project updates and latest news, EtherWorld is one of the leading blockchain content publishing websites. In 2020, during the preparation of the launch of Ethereum 2.0 (Beacon chain), [BlockAction](https://github.com/blockaction) was launched as an ephimeral explorer to support Beacon chain multiclient testnet since the launch of Witti testnet. The explorer was upgraded to provide information of the Beaconchain mainnet transaction and was discontinued in Jan 2022. Pooja has been actively contributing to the Ethereum Improvement Proposal Improvement Process (EIPIP) and EIP data analytics. She publishes a monthly [EIPs stats. report](https://hackmd.io/@poojaranjan/EthereumImprovementProposalsInsight). To extend continued support to the Ethereum community, she is all set to launch the next project [EIPsInsight](https://eipsinsight.com/), a data analytics dashboard for EIPs. Pooja Ranjan is the Herder-in-chief of [Ethereum Cat Herders](https://www.ethereumcatherders.com/), a decentralized project management group to support Ethereum Protocol. She is the host of a popular education series [PEEPanEIP](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4cwHXAawZxqu0PKKyMzG_3BJV_xZTi1F).
